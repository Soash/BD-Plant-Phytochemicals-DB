Acknowledgement

The development of the Bangladeshi Medicinal Plant Phytochemicals Database (BMPPD) has been made possible through the collective effort, leadership, and technical expertise of individuals involved in the planning, development, and coordination of the project. The Project Director provided strategic leadership, research direction, and overall supervision to ensure the scientific quality and long-term vision of the initiative, while the Project Manager oversaw project planning, timelines, and resource management to ensure efficient execution and effective team coordination. The Deputy Project Managers supported operational oversight, progress monitoring, and quality assurance throughout the development phases, and the Project Coordinators facilitated communication among research, technical, and documentation teams while supporting data curation and system integration activities. The Database Developer played a crucial role in designing, developing, and maintaining the database architecture, ensuring data integrity, system performance, usability, and secure access to curated phytochemical information. The dedication and contributions of all individuals involved are sincerely acknowledged, as their combined efforts have been essential in delivering a reliable, research-oriented database that supports scientific discovery and innovation.
