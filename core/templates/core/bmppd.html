What is BMPPD?

Bangladeshi Medicinal Plant Phytochemicals Database

BMPPD – Bangladeshi Medicinal Plant Phytochemicals Database is the first curated phytochemical database of medicinal plants in Bangladesh, developed through two years of dedicated research at Dawnilab, the research hub of Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited, Bangladesh.

Dawnilab focuses on transforming scientific knowledge into practical innovations. BMPPD embodies this vision by bridging research and application, providing a structured and reliable resource for natural product exploration.

The database currently covers more than 700 medicinal plants and over 120,000 phytochemical compounds, curated from a wide range of peer-reviewed scientific publications.

Why BMPPD?

BMPPD provides a centralized platform for researchers, academicians, and pharmaceutical developers to explore the rich medicinal plant diversity of Bangladesh.

By integrating cheminformatics and bioinformatics tools, BMPPD enables:

  • Accelerated natural product research and drug discovery
  • Systems-level analysis of phytochemical action mechanisms
  • Easy access to curated, high-quality, and structured plant–compound data
