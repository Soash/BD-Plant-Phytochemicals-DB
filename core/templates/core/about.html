About BMPPD

The Bangladeshi Medicinal Plant Phytochemicals Database (BMPPD) is a research-focused digital resource developed to support the systematic study of medicinal plants and their phytochemical constituents in Bangladesh. It represents the first curated phytochemical database of Bangladeshi medicinal plants, created through two years of dedicated data collection, validation, and organisation.

BMPPD provides a centralised, structured, and searchable platform containing information on more than 700 medicinal plants and over 120,000 phytochemical compounds, curated from peer-reviewed scientific literature. The database is designed to assist researchers, students, and pharmaceutical scientists in natural product research, phytochemistry, and early-stage drug discovery.

All information in BMPPD is intended strictly for research and educational purposes, with continuous improvement supported through expert review and user feedback.

About Dawnilab

Dawnilab is the research and innovation hub of Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh. It focuses on transforming scientific knowledge into practical, data-driven solutions by integrating bioinformatics, cheminformatics, and computational research methodologies.

The core mission of Dawnilab is to develop high-quality research resources, analytical platforms, and digital tools that support life science research and innovation. BMPPD is one of Dawnilab’s flagship research initiatives, reflecting its commitment to data accuracy, transparency, and scientific impact.

Dawnilab supports interdisciplinary collaboration and emphasizes responsible research practices aligned with global scientific standards.

About Dawn of Bioinformatics Ltd. Bangladesh

Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh represents a pioneering force in the nation’s scientific community as the first company to seamlessly connect the complexities of bioinformatics with practical, real-world applications. We serve as a beacon for researchers, offering a wide range of services including genomics, proteomics, metagenomics, immunoinformatics, transcriptomics, systems biology, and synthetic biology. Founded by a visionary researcher, our company plays a pivotal role in supporting the pharmaceutical and research industries, particularly in the realms of drug and vaccine design. Through initiatives like the Bioinformatics Skill Development Scheme, we empower students with critical expertise and foster international collaborations, advancing the bioinformatics landscape both within Bangladesh and globally.
